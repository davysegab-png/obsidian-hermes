# RE Bot — Outreach Templates

**For:** Reaching out to realtor Facebook friends
**Goal:** Get a 20-minute call to demo the bot
**Tone:** Casual, friendly, low-pressure. You're a friend asking for feedback, not a salesperson.

---

## Template 1 — The Casual Ask

> Hey [Name]! Haven't talked in a while — hope you're doing good.
>
> Quick thing: I've been building AI tools on the side and one of them is a personal assistant specifically for real estate agents. It automatically follows up with your leads, sends you morning briefings, schedules showings — all through a Telegram bot.
>
> I'm looking for 2-3 agents to test it with me and give honest feedback. No charge, no commitment — I just want real feedback before I start charging people.
>
> Would you be up for a 20-min call this week to take a look? It'd really help me out.

---

## Template 2 — The FOMO Ask

> Hey [Name] — hope you're crushing it in real estate!
>
> I built something I think you'd actually like — it's a Telegram bot that acts like your personal assistant for leads and showings. You text it naturally and it handles the follow-up stuff so you don't miss opportunities.
>
> I'm offering free access for a month to [2] agents I trust to give real feedback. Would you be one of them? 20 min of your time to check it out.

## After They Say Yes — Book the Call

> Awesome, thanks [Name]!
>
> Here's how this works: I'll send you a link to the Telegram bot, you text it once to make sure it works, then we jump on a 20-min call so I can walk you through it and you can tell me what you think.
>
> What day/time works for you this week?

---

## When Offering the Pilot → Close

> So — what do you think? Does that actually solve a problem for you?
>
> Cool. Here's the deal: I'm offering the first month free so you can really feel it out. After that it's $[500]/month — cancel anytime.
>
> You in?

---

## Key Rules

1. **Always ask for the call, not the sale.** The call is the close.
2. **Never use the word "AI" in the first message.** Say "assistant" or "bot."
3. **Never pitch features.** Pitch the outcome: "so you don't miss a lead."
4. **Always give an out.** "No pressure, no strings."
5. **Follow up once after 3 days.** Then stop.
