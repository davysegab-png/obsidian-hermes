# Baby Duck Stories — SIMPLE versions
*Three original stories for a little one who loves being Baby Duck*

---

## Story 1: Baby Duck Goes UP

Baby duck saw a hill.
A BIG hill.
"I can't go up that!" said baby duck.

Taytay duck said:
"Yes you can. One step. Then another. Then another."

Baby duck went ONE step.
Then ANOTHER.
Then ANOTHER.

He went UP and UP and UP.

He did it!
"I DID IT!" said baby duck.
"You DID IT!" said taytay duck.
Baby duck laughed all the way down.

---

## Story 2: Splash Splash Splash

Baby duck had a bath.
A BIG bath.
SPLASH! Baby duck kicked.
SPLASH! Water went on taytay duck.
SPLASH! More water! MORE!

Baby duck laughed.
Taytay duck laughed.
The rubber ducks went BOING BOING BOING.

"Again! AGAIN!" said baby duck.
"Again!" said taytay duck.
SPLASH!

---

## Story 3: The Storm

One night there was a storm.
BOOM! went the thunder.
Baby duck was scared.
"I want taytay!" said baby duck.

Taytay came.
"I am here," said taytay.
Baby duck held on.
Taytay held on.

They went to the door.
They looked at the storm.
The storm went BOOM.

Baby duck was still scared.
But he was with taytay.
So he was okay.
So he was brave.

Baby duck went back to bed.
He slept all night.
